Our Mission

The Consolata Seminary is dedicated to the formation of future Church leaders, emphasizing the integration of academic excellence, spiritual formation, and service to the community. We strive to foster a vibrant faith and a compassionate leadership style that impacts both the Church and the world.

We are committed to providing a holistic education grounded in Christian values, enabling our seminarians to grow spiritually, intellectually, and emotionally as they prepare for their ministries.

Church & World Affairs

Our seminary is deeply committed to engaging with global and local affairs that challenge and shape the Church’s mission in the world. We train seminarians to be leaders who are not only theologically grounded but also socially and politically aware of the issues impacting the lives of the people they serve.
